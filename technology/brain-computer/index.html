Technologies that detect and interpret brain activity in order to provide user inputs to computing devices, and potentially provide input to influence brain activity.

Example applications

Enabling those with paralysis to operate equipment.

Supporting intuitive and near instantaneous option selection (thought-to-command)

Emotional sensing and mood management

Providing neurocognitive feedback to enhance cognitive performance

Time to Effect (most optimistic) : 3 Years

Time to Effect (most likely) : 10 Years

Time to Effect (most pessimistic) : 20 Years
